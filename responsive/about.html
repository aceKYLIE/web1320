Behavior of a crow

Crows are verry social birds. Crows are known to flock together, and sometimes they can be seen flocking in 1000s. crows are extremely smart. crows are also known to be excellent learners and problem solvers; however, this has given them the reputation of being mischievous. They can also be aggressive when provoked. Often times they will fight other birds that are known to be larger and stronger such as hawks and owls.
