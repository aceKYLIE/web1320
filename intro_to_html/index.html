Origin of the Black Cats Bad Luck

Fear of black cats arose in the middle ages. This was because they have nocturnal habits, and the fact you could only see there glowing eyes in the dark. It was also a common belief that the form of a black cat was a favorite amongst transforming witches, who flew around bringing misfortune into the lives of those around them. Pope Gregory IX publicly said that black cats where of the devil. In the united states, merly owning a black cat was punishable by hanging during the Salem Witch trials.
